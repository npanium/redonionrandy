Shop Artwork

These are artworks straight from Red Onion State Prison, designed by Randy and other prison inmates.

Part of the proceeds go towards helping prisoners like Randy get representation. We also support charities that fight for social justice.

"Philosophy"

“This piece is a combination of New Orleans and Chinese philosophy. The star shaped patterns within this piece combined with the human emotions is symbolic with our relationship within the cosmos. It’s our struggle to rise above the daily toil in our lives, to feel that life is grander and that we have a place in that design. All of us want and need to know that the things we go through in life are worth it, that our pain serves a purpose.”

"Nice Person"

“He was such a nice person before he went to American Prison”

"Fluffy Kitten"

“What did you expect after a decade in solitary? A fluffy kitten?”
