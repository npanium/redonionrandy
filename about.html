About

In my podcast I talk about what it’s like to live in one of the toughest prisons in America without the possibility of parole. I’ve already spent more than 20 years here, 14 of them in solitary confinement. I take responsibility for the crime I committed and the choices I make today. I’m not the angry young guy I once was. I try to be a blessing and not a curse, and share what I’ve learned with others. That’s why I hope you’ll check out my podcasts. I invite your questions and comments and will respond in upcoming episodes.

-Randy
